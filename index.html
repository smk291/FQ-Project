Hi.

This website uses mapping/Google directions and granular weather forecasting to determine not just what the whether will be but where users will likely be when it changes.


The webapp is powered by a combination of forecast.io and various Google maps apis, and (eventually) charts.js and weatherunderground's map overlays.


Start by clicking the "Plan a trip" tab above, and then enter your origin and destination. The app will plot your course, mark your likely progress as time passes, and tell you what the weather will be at those times/places.
