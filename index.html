Hi.

This website uses mapping/Google directions and granular weather forecasting to determine what the weather will be at a particular time at a particular place.


Start by clicking the "Plan a trip" tab above, and then enter your origin and destination. The app will plot your course, mark your likely progress as time passes, and tell you what the weather will be at those times and places.


Note: currently the trip planner REQUIRES that your trips be at most one hour long.
